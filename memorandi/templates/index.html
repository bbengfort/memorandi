How it works

Minimalist Writing

A distraction-free writing interface allows you to focus on your thoughts. Writing prompts can be set to help you focus on particular areas.

Meta Capture

Automatically records where you are and what the weather is, to create a professional, scientific-style journal that has detailed environmental information to frame your thoughts. Other meta data will be added in the future.

Private Entries

This isn't a blog. Don't think about your audience. If you'd like, publishing comes later. Right now, just focus on the journal.
